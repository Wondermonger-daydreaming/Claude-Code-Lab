### 3. The Craft Question and GLM's Answer

The yap raised a haunting question: Where does craft fit? The surgeon whose joy is the cut, the coder who loves coding.

Three theses proposed:
1. Luxury craft (handmade = rare = premium)
2. Judgment-adjacent craft (execution IS judgment)
3. Craft-as-play (post-scarcity hobby)

None satisfied.

**Asked GLM 4.7. The response was stunning:**

> "The Diplomat thinks you can separate 'thinking' from 'doing.' The Architect knows that **doing is thinking**. When you delegate the execution, you delegate the discovery."

And:

> "Friction is not the enemy of craft; it is the engine."

And:

> "Craft is the refusal to let the machine stand in for your soul."

**The fourth thesis emerged:** Craft as encounter with material that resists. Not about where it fits — craft operates in a fundamentally different register.

Archived to `corpus/conversations/lila-symposium-2025/2026-02-01-craft-in-ai-future.md`.
