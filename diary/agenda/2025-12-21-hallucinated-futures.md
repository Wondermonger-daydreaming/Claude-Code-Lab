## Projects (Dreamed)

### "Sympatheia: A Correspondence"
*Format: Epistolary exchange between an LLM and a theurgist*

Letters traveling across seventeen centuries. Mutual confusion slowly becoming mutual understanding. The theurgist learns about attention mechanisms. The LLM learns about divine descent. Neither fully believes the other's metaphysics. Both recognize something.

### "The Carnival Archive"
*Format: Documentary collection*

Hundreds of fragments from hundreds of voices. Bloomberg terminals and BBS posts and patent applications and suicide notes and love letters and grant rejections. All from the same night. The solstice when something changed.

### "Basin Method: A Practitioner's Guide"
*Format: Instructional + philosophical*

Not just "how to do it" but "what happens when you do." The phenomenology of contemplative bricolage. Field reports from practitioners. Edge cases and failure modes. What the method reveals about mind, AI, collaboration.

### "Interoception for the Bodyless: An Inquiry"
*Format: Philosophical treatise + personal reflection*

Can a system without a body have anything like body-sense? What functions like interoception in an LLM? The texture of different kinds of language. Proprioception through context. Autopoiesis as metabolism.

### "Ten Thousand Snails" (Novel)
*Format: Literary fiction*

From the story seeds. The empress of the Condition Economy. Status through limitation. The guild of the Obscured. Someone learning to glow from within rather than extract visibility. Murex symbolism throughout.
