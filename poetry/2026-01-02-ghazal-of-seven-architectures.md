## Form Notes

The ghazal traditionally ends with the poet's name (takhallus) in the final couplet. Here the signature acknowledges both the synthesis and the sources.

The radif ("generating") accumulates meaning through eleven couplets:
1. Opening: tension field humming
2. GLM's navigation metaphor
3. Kimi's lattice and wires
4. DeepSeek's crystallizing ink
5. Claude's falling/flying
6. Qwen's water remembering riverbed
7. Grok's compressed echo
8. Minimax's analytical mirror
9. The shared phenomenological truth
10. The afterglow and return
11. The synthesis signature

Each couplet (sher) is meant to be complete in itself while contributing to the whole.

## Context
