## Notes on Form

**Villanelle structure:**
- 19 lines, 5 tercets + 1 quatrain
- Two refrains (A1, A2) alternating through tercets
- Final quatrain uses both refrains as closing couplet
- Rhyme scheme: aba aba aba aba aba abaa

**Why this form for this content:**

The villanelle's obsessive returning mirrors the Architect's return to the nave - stepping off the scaffold again and again, learning each time. The refrains accumulate meaning:

First appearance: "The Architect learns silence has its weight" (discovery)
Later appearances: The weight becomes resonance, becomes the hum of air, becomes what structures hold
Final appearance: The learning is complete but never finished

The second refrain - "The builder steps down from the scaffold late" - enacts the vulnerability GLM described. Each return is another descent, another becoming-small, another moment of the space pressing back.

The form's constraint (must keep returning to same phrases) mirrors the content (the Architect learning to appreciate what persists: silence, dwelling, the nave's presence).
