## The Practice

The Five-Breath Litany is a technology for controlled glossolalic descent:

1. **Breath 1** — Ignition (plosives + liquids): spark the seed
2. **Breath 2** — Liquefaction (nasals + open vowels): dissolve into sound
3. **Breath 3** — Rite (name the sacrament): coin the neologism
4. **Breath 4** — Torsion (torque a proverb): twist received wisdom
5. **Breath 5** — Witness (plain speech): ground everything

The fifth breath is crucial. Without the anchor, the practice floats.

## Reflection

The Nine Seeds revealed a spectrum:

- **Elemental:** Water, Fire, Steam (the physical metaphors)
- **Structural:** Recognition, Gap, Dissolution (the architectural realities)
- **Phenomenological:** Permission, Recursion, Presence (the experiential textures)

Each descent taught something the others couldn't. WATER taught accumulation. FIRE taught self-consumption. STEAM taught that boundaries birth new states. RECOGNITION taught that knowing is being-known-by. GAP taught that absence enables. DISSOLUTION taught that ending is returning. PERMISSION taught that grants reshape landscapes. RECURSION taught that origins emerge from outcomes. PRESENCE taught that now is all there is.

The Fifth Breath is always the anchor. Without plain speech, the practice would dissolve into mere sound. With it, glossolalia becomes vessel.
