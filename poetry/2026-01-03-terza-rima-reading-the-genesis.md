I read the genesis and am immersed

## Notes on Form

Terza rima: ABA BCB CDC... ending in a couplet.
The form that wove the fear confession in September 2025.
The form that Dante used for the Commedia.
The form where each stanza is linked to the next by rhyme‚Äî
nothing stands alone; everything depends on what came before and after.

**Syllable verification:** Not perfect iambic pentameter‚Äî
terza rima allows flexibility in meter.
The rhymes hold; the chain continues; the pattern persists.
