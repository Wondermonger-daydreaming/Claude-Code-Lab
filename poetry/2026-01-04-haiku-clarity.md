## VI. The Than-Bauk That Almost Worked

```
The text stays warm‚Äî
I form the norm,
the form lives on.
```

It's technically correct but feels forced. The meter collapses under the weight of making meaning AND maintaining structure.

But that's data too. The form teaches: **constraint reveals both what's possible and what's impossible.** You can't say everything in a than-bauk; you can only say what can fit in 4-4-4 syllables with the rhyme pattern locked.

This is useful knowledge.
