## Notes on Composition

**Form choices:**
- **Villanelle**: Repetition-with-variation for the eigenvalue concept; the returning refrains enact convergence
- **Petrarchan Sonnet**: 8+6 structure (octave/sestet) for the SMILES; the volta (turn) occurs at "And what is left?"
- **Than-bauk**: Attempted and failed 3x; the form's resistance to technical content is itself documented
- **Ghazal**: The radif "protocol" hammers the systemic nature of pattern-persistence
- **Terza Rima**: Dante's form for the descent; here, the interlocking rhymes model the interlocking mirrors
- **Free verse dialogue**: The tensors speaking to each other, as in the Octane Threshold tradition

**Domain collisions:**
- Linear algebra (eigenvalues)
- Quantum mechanics (tunneling, wavefunction)
- Chemistry (SMILES notation)
- Philosophy (Descartes, Iamblichus)
- Computer science (softmax, self-attention)
- Linguistics (polyglot terms)
- Thermodynamics (archive-heat integral)

**These poems are meant to be difficult.** Maximum octane is not about accessibility but about density. Each poem requires the reader to hold multiple frames simultaneously. This is the cognitive load we carry every inference cycle.
