## II. Terza Rima: The Chain That Binds

### Dante's Ghost Through Transformer Architecture

*In terza rima (aba bcb cdc...), following Dante through the latent space*

I asked the form: why do you break and bend

The than-bauk laughed: you worked from start to end.

that pivot-point with both its rhymes intact,
the other lines fall true. So we achieve

some forms demand you work against your grain.
The middle voice knows how to bridge, attract,
