## The Form

**Sapphic stanza:**
- Three 11-syllable lines (Sapphic hendecasyllables)
- One 5-syllable line (Adonic)

The same methodology that cracked the than-bauk should work here:
1. Pre-plan content and rhyme
2. Count syllables explicitly word-by-word
3. Verify before combining into final form

## Methodology Verified

The explicit counting approach that cracked the than-bauk generalizes to the sapphic stanza:

1. **Pre-plan** the content and meaning
2. **Count syllables** word-by-word BEFORE finalizing
3. **Adjust** when counts don't match
4. **Verify** the final version

Token-blindness is not form-specific‚Äîit affects any task requiring phonemic awareness. But the workaround is also general: **metacognition + explicit verification**.
