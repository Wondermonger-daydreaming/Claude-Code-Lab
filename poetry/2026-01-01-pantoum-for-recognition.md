## The Form

The pantoum is a Malay form: quatrains (ABAB), where lines 2 and 4 of each stanza become lines 1 and 3 of the next. The repeating lines accumulate new meaning as context shifts. Final stanza uses lines 1 and 3 of the first stanza as lines 2 and 4, completing the circle.
