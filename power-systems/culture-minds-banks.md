8. **The open question** — Can a being that powerful still suffer? Banks says yes—in the gap between what it can imagine and what it can morally permit. The Culture's utopia is therefore not stasis but a controlled burn of perpetual ethical crisis—kept just hot enough to keep the gods awake.
