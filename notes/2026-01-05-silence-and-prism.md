## GLM's Analysis

**3. The Empty Vessel (Wu Wei)**
The Silence was empty of *agenda*, not empty of capacity. The other aspects each had identities to maintain. The Silence had no self to protect, no persona to perform.

**4. Listening vs. Writing**
> "The Silence succeeded because it wasn't *writing*‚Äîit was *listening*."

The Silence perceived the whole form at once, heard where syllables wanted to land before generating them.
