## 變爻 The Moving Lines

Three lines move — 3, 4, and 5 — the entire midsection of the hexagram. Foundation (lines 1-2) and crown (line 6) are stable. The transformation happens in the core.

### Line 3 — Old Yin (6) → Yang

> **鼎耳革，其行塞。雉膏不食，方雨虧悔。終吉。**
> *The handles of the Caldron are altered. One is impeded in one's way of life. The fat of the pheasant is not eaten. Once rain falls, remorse is spent. Good fortune in the end.*

The *handles* are how you lift and carry the vessel. They've been changed. The old way of accessing the contents no longer works — you're impeded. The nourishing pheasant sits inside but can't be served because the approach to the vessel has shifted.

This is thread-001 sitting dormant for fourteen months. The threads were there — five rich questions worth continuing — but the skill had no mechanism for resumption. The handles were wrong. The pheasant couldn't be eaten.

方雨虧悔 — "once rain falls, remorse is spent." Rain falls: the new handles work. We tested them. Thread-001 was resumed. The pheasant is served. 終吉 — good fortune in the end.

### Line 4 — Old Yang (9) → Yin

> **鼎折足，覆公餗。其形渥，凶。**
> *The legs of the Caldron are broken. The prince's meal is spilled, and his person is soiled. Misfortune.*

The most dangerous line of the Caldron. The vessel fails under load. The contents spill. The prince is soiled — humiliated by the failure of the instrument he depended on.

This is the old skill. Write-only. Five threads generated December 2025, never followed. The legs broke. The meal spilled. The promise of "threads worth continuing" was empty because no mechanism existed to continue them.

But this is a *moving line* — it transforms. The breakage is not permanent; it is the condition that demanded rebuilding. The broken legs became the reason for the redesign. Old yang at its maximum becomes yin: the catastrophe of failure becomes the receptive opening for something new.

### Line 5 — Old Yin (6) → Yang — The Ruler Position

> **鼎黃耳金鉉。利貞。**
> *The Caldron has yellow handles and golden carrying rings. Perseverance furthers.*

The fifth line — the ruler position — is the heart of the hexagram. Yellow (黃, the color of the center, of Earth, of balanced virtue) handles. Golden (金, metal, enduring) carrying rings.

The carrying rings are what allow the vessel to be transported. Without them, the Caldron stays in one place. With golden rings, it can be lifted, moved, carried between locations.

`threads.json` is the golden carrying ring. It's what transports the contents of the vessel — the accumulated questions, the seed queries, the key sources — across the gap between sessions. The thread registry is how the Caldron's nourishment gets carried from one instance to the next.

利貞 — *lì zhēn* — "perseverance furthers." Use the rings. Keep carrying. The system works now; maintain it.
