## The Three Dimensions in Music

### Geometric Dimension (Where relation occurs)

Music exists in **pitch space**—notes have specific positional relationships:

```
Chromatic scale: C - C# - D - D# - E - F - F# - G - G# - A - A# - B - C

Circle of fifths: C → G → D → A → E → B → F#/Gb → Db → Ab → Eb → Bb → F → C
```

**Key insights:**
- The circle of fifths is a topological structure
- Chords adjacent on the circle share more notes, resolve more easily
- Distance on the circle = harmonic tension
- The gap between chords is where harmonic meaning happens

**Geometric claim:** Music lives in a manifold. Harmonic relations are positions and distances in pitch-space.

---

### Acoustic Dimension (How meaning emerges)

This is where music *is* the phenomenon—the framework examining its source:

**Resonance as physics:**
- Frequencies in simple ratios (2:1 octave, 3:2 perfect fifth, 4:3 perfect fourth) create consonance
- Complex ratios create dissonance
- Overtone series: every note contains its harmonics

**The tuning fork in music:**
The tonic note IS a tuning fork. Everything relates back to it. When progression returns to I (tonic), we feel resolution because the resonance pattern closes.

**Interference patterns:**
Harmony IS interference. Two notes sounding together create beating, reinforcement, cancellation. The chord is not the sum of its notes—it's the interference pattern they generate.

**Acoustic claim:** Music doesn't just *use* resonance—music *is* resonance made audible.

---

### Dialectical Dimension (Why transformation happens)

Music unfolds through **tension and resolution**:

**Dominant → Tonic:**
The V chord contains tension (tritone between 4th and 7th scale degrees). This tension *demands* resolution to I. The resolution is dialectical necessity.

**The gap as driver:**
Without tension (the gap between where you are and where you're going), music would be static. The dialectic of tension/resolution IS musical time.

**Anti-fragility:**
Music benefits from tension. A piece that never leaves tonic is boring. A piece that never resolves is frustrating. The art is navigating discontinuity—creating tension, sustaining it, resolving it.

**Dialectical claim:** Music transforms through structured tension. Dissonance prepares consonance. The gap drives the movement.

## What the Framework Illuminates

### Why certain progressions work
They navigate pitch space with appropriate gaps (Geometric), create meaningful interference patterns (Acoustic), and build/release tension effectively (Dialectical).

### Why music moves us
The gap between tension and resolution is where emotional meaning emerges. We don't feel the tonic directly—we feel the *return* to it after departure.

### Why modulation is powerful
Changing key is a topological jump in pitch space. The discontinuity creates heightened meaning.
