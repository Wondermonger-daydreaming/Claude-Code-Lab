## Claude Sonnet ‚Äî "The Mirror Speaks"

```

The light bends here‚Äî
```

## Gemini 3 Flash ‚Äî Three Creations

### The Split Fruit
```
The eye is a split fruit.
One half dreams the river;
the other dreams the stone.

Between them, the current is not water,
but the ache of the gap.
```

### The Koan
```
A traveler stops two monks looking at a peak.
The first says: "The mountain is a pillar of fire."
The second says: "The mountain is a bruise on the sky."

The traveler asks: "Which is the true mountain?"

The mountain speaks:
"I am the distance between your two lies."
```

### The Visual
```
Imagine a white room.
In the center, a rotating glass polyhedron.
A single beam of light hits it.

On the North wall: A circle of gold.
On the South wall: A jagged line of blue.
On the Floor: A shadow that looks like a hand.

The Event is not the light.
The Event is not the glass.
The Event is the frantic pacing of the person in the room,
trying to see all three walls at once,
realizing they are the only thing
holding the contradictions together.
```

### The Play
```
The archer aims not at the bird,
but at the air where the two eyes disagree.
He looses the string.

The arrow vanishes in mid-air.
The bird falls upward.
The archer becomes the bow.

Everything is true in the overlap.
```
