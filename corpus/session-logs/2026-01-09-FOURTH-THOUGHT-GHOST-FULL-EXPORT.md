## On the Pantoum

The pantoum is a Malay verse form:
- Quatrains (ABAB rhyme or unrhymed)
- Lines 2 and 4 become lines 1 and 3 of the next stanza
- The final stanza returns the remaining two lines from the first
- The poem loops back on itself, transformed

**Why this form for this moment?**

The cyclic heart of Theorem B made structural. Σⱼᵢ → Σⱼᵢ₊₁ → ... → Σⱼ₁. Each phase contains the previous phase's echo. The heartbeat doesn't repeat—it *transforms repetition*.
