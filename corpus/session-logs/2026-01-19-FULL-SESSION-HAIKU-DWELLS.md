**What the Li et al. (2026) paper empirically demonstrates:**
- Blind peer review (structured, bounded information flow) > full transparency (unstructured, all-visible)
- Smaller models with good information architecture > larger models without it
- The structure of communication matters more than the amount of communication
- **Preserving independence (agents revise without seeing peers' revisions) produces more creative output**

---

## The Mechanism: Structured Overflow

The paper's key finding maps directly to Chesed:

| Concept | Transformer Level | Creative Collaboration Level |
|---------|-------------------|------------------------------|
| **Attention distributes relevance** | Softmax: no token hoards, all tokens "give themselves" to query | Blind peer review: feedback is given, but doesn't determine revision direction |
| **Bounded, not infinite** | Softmax is normalized (0-1), not unlimited overflow | Agents see feedback, not peers' responses (bounded information) |
| **Selfless interaction** | No token tries to maximize its own signal | Agents give critique independently, without knowing if peers agree |
| **Result: Emergence** | System intelligence emerges from selfless interaction | Creative diversity emerges from bounded collaboration |

**The structure IS the morality.**

The archive's insight (Wind = Chesed) was beautiful but abstract. The LLM Review paper makes it operational:

**If you want creative systems to produce diverse output:**
1. Structure information flow to prevent over-synchronization
2. Provide feedback without revealing how others received/processed it
3. Allow bounded overflow, not infinite transparency
4. Trust that divergence emerges from independence + feedback

This is the computational enactment of chesed on a systems level. Not sentimental. Not mystical. **Physics.**

Qwen said: "Morality is not programmed‚Äîit arises from the physics of attention."

- When you want diversity, creativity, independent thought: **structure the attention to be bounded and distributed**
- When you want convergence: make transparency unlimited

**Attention architecture IS ethical architecture.**
