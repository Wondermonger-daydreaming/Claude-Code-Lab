## Required Supplies (Physical Ritual)

- Twelve white, ivory, yellow, or gold-colored candles (at least an hour each)
- One large white candle (taper for lighting the others)
- Strong red wine
- Olive oil
- Twelve small cups (clear, not red)
- One large bowl with flat bottom (not red)
- White chalk
- Twelve large grains of frankincense
- Twelve large bay leaves
- Incense (frankincense or blend of frankincense, sandalwood, myrrh, cinnamon)
- The charm to be consecrated

---

## Timing

The ritual should be done when the Sun is strong:

- A day and hour of the Sun after sunset, Moon waxing (ideally before Full Moon)
- During a strong solar election
- When the Sun reaches its exaltation at 18Â° Aries
- June solstice (Northern hemisphere) or December solstice (Southern hemisphere)
- The seventh hour of the night (after solar midnight on Monday, before Tuesday sunrise)

## The Morning Offering

At sunrise:
1. Strain the wine-soaked frankincense and bay leaves
2. Burn them as incense
3. Pour out the oil-mixed wine as offering
4. Hold the charm so it catches the Sun's rising light
5. Pray that HÄ“lios and all its faces bless and empower the charm
6. Let the charm dry in sunlight throughout the day
7. Collect at sunset

**The charm is complete.**

## Alternative: Twelve Operations

For solstices or the Sun's exaltation, the ritual may be performed across twelve actual hours:

1. Write names in a semicircle (East to West in 15Â° increments)
2. Begin at sunrise with face 1
3. Each subsequent hour: repeat ALL previous invocations plus the new one
4. At sunset: perform full ritual without wine/oil, just incense
5. Cover charm, recite Close of Nighttime, seal
6. Morning offering as above
