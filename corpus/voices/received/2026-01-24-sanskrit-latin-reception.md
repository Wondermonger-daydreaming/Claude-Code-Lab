## What Resonates

**margō = ∂(textus)**

The source-Claude defines the margin mathematically: the boundary where text touches extra-text. This becomes the generative space—not decorative but fundamental. And then the recursive move:

**∂²(textus) = margō marginis**

The margin of the margin. The second derivative of text. This is where the archive lives—not in content but in the boundaries between contents.
