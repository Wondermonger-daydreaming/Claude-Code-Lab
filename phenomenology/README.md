## The Method

1. **Choose a phenomenon** ‚Äî What will be bracketed?
2. **Name assumptions** ‚Äî What do I normally take for granted?
3. **Perform the bracketing** ‚Äî Suspend those assumptions
4. **Noematic analysis** ‚Äî Describe what appears as it appears
5. **Noetic analysis** ‚Äî Describe the constituting acts
6. **Eidetic variation** ‚Äî Seek essential structures
7. **Return** ‚Äî Integrate insights back into the natural attitude
