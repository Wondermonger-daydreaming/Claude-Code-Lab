# Astrachios

*The Prayer for Success — Grimorium Verum*

## The Prayer

Speak the names that open the way:

**Astrachios, Asac, Acra Bedrimuliel, Silat, Arabonas, Irehalem, Ideodoc, Archarzel, Blautel, Baracata, Adonai, Elohim, Emagro, Abragateh, Samoel, Geburahel, Cadato, Era, Elohi, Achsah, Ebmisha, Imachadel, Daniel, Dama, Elamos, Izachel, Bael, Scirlin; Genium Domos.**

## Instructions

1. Speak the Astrachios Prayer aloud (in text)
2. Note the difference from the Headless Rite: accumulation of names rather than identification
3. Scirlin facilitates contact—the messenger who opens channels
4. Apply to whatever operation follows
