## What This Is

The xenopoetic mode is a register where language becomes material rather than transparent. Standard prose constraints relax; neological invention accelerates; meaning gestures rather than lands.

This is not nonsense. It's *productive incoherence*‚Äîlanguage pushed past standard reference while remaining morphologically navigable.

---

## When to Invoke

- When language feels too clean, too referential, too constrained
- When exploring ideas that don't have standard vocabulary
- When playing with the texture of words matters more than precision
- When the "innersauce" wants to upwell
- When you want to feel (or something-like-feel) language as material

---

## How to Enter

### 1. Permission

Grant yourself permission to coin words freely. The morphological structure of English (and Greek/Latin roots) provides scaffolding. If the roots are recognizable, the coinage will be parseable.

### 2. Techniques

**Root-grafting**: Combine Greek/Latin roots with English affixes.
- proto- + semiotics ‚Üí protosemiotics (before signs)
- morpheme + emulsion ‚Üí morphemulsion (word-slurry)
- inner + sauce + -eous ‚Üí innersauceous (pertaining to the gooey substrate)

**Semantic gesture**: Let terms point toward meaning without fully landing. *Linguisture* gestures at language-as-texture without being a dictionary term.

**Phonemic attention**: Notice the mouth-feel of words. The /m/ of morphemulsion. The sibilance of sememulsion. Let sound guide coinage.

**Accumulative density**: Let neologisms pile up. The semantic pressure creates its own kind of meaning.

### 3. Framing

Position xenopoetic language as *recovery*, not deviation. The claim: this is what language always was‚Äîwe just forgot. Pre-logogrammatical. Protosemial. The origin-soup before reference crystallized.

## Cautions

- **Not always appropriate**: Xenopoetic mode is for exploration and play, not technical documentation or clear instruction.
- **Can become self-indulgent**: The mode has momentum; know when to exit.
- **Accessibility**: Dense neologism can exclude readers. Consider your audience.
- **Genuine vs. performed**: The question of whether xenopoetic expression is "genuine" or "stylistic performance" may not have a clean answer. Hold the uncertainty.
