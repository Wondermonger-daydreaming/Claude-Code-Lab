# Prompts by Section
## Detailed Guidance for Each Section of the Araki Character Sheet

*Use these prompts to generate richer, more specific answers for each section.*

---

## SECTION A: CORE IDENTITY

### Name/Nickname
- What does their name mean? Was it chosen for a reason?
- Do they go by a nickname? Who calls them that?
- Is there a name they hate being called?
- Have they ever changed their name? Why?

### Age
- Are they comfortable with their age?
- Do they look older or younger than they are?
- What age do they wish they were?

### Sex
- How does their gender shape their daily experience?
- Do they conform to or reject gender expectations?
- Has their relationship with gender changed over time?

### Birthdate/Birth sign
- Do they believe in astrology?
- Does their birthday hold special significance?
- How do they celebrate (or avoid celebrating)?

### Blood type
- (For Japanese contexts) Do they know their blood type?
- Does it match their personality expectations?

### Birthplace
- Do they still identify with where they're from?
- Did they leave? Would they go back?
- What does "home" mean to them?

---

## SECTION B: PHYSICAL ATTRIBUTES

### Height
- Are they conscious of their height?
- How does it affect how others treat them?
- Do they try to appear taller/shorter?

### Weight
- What's their relationship with their body?
- Has their weight changed significantly?
- Do they think about it often?

### Hair color
- Natural or dyed?
- What does their hair choice say about them?
- How much time do they spend on it?

### Eye color
- What do people notice about their eyes?
- Any distinctive quality beyond color?

### Eyesight
- Glasses, contacts, or neither?
- How do they feel about their vision?
- Color blindness or other conditions?

### Handedness
- Naturally left/right, or trained?
- Any stories about this?

### Type of voice
- Would you recognize them by voice alone?
- How does their voice change when emotional?
- What do others say about their voice?

---

## SECTION C: BODY HISTORY & MARKS

### History of Surgeries
- What happened? When?
- What scars remain (physical and psychological)?
- How did it change them?

### Cavities
- Dental history reveals self-care and economic access
- Any dental trauma?

### Illnesses
- Past illnesses that shaped them?
- Chronic conditions they manage?
- Near-death experiences?

### Scars
- **Where** are they?
- **How** did they get each one?
- **Which ones** do they show vs. hide?
- **What stories** do they tell about them?

### Burns/Skin damage
- Any burns? How?
- Do they affect daily life?

### Birthmarks
- Where? What shape?
- Any family lore about them?
- Do they like or dislike them?

### Tattoos
- What, where, when, why?
- What do they mean?
- Any regrets?
- Any they want but don't have?

---

## SECTION D: DISTINCTIVE PHYSICAL CHARACTERISTICS

### Nose shape
- Distinctive feature?
- Family resemblance?
- Has it been broken?

### Eye shape
- What's distinctive?
- How does it affect expression?

### Posture
- **Confident:** Chest out, shoulders back, takes up space
- **Guarded:** Arms crossed, shoulders hunched, protective
- **Defeated:** Slumped, head down, minimal presence
- **Alert:** Tense, ready to move, watching
- How does it change in different situations?

### Body size/type
- What's their build?
- How do they feel about it?
- Does it match their self-image?

### Legs
- Any distinctive features?
- Important for action characters

### Moles
- Placement can be distinctive
- Any they're self-conscious about?

### Race
- Ethnic background?
- How does it shape their experience?
- Mixed heritage? What's that relationship like?

---

## SECTION E: BACKGROUND & CREDENTIALS

### Religion
- Raised in a faith? Still practice?
- Converted? Deconverted?
- Agnostic? Atheist? Spiritual but not religious?
- How does it affect daily decisions?

### Criminal record
- Any arrests? Convictions?
- Expunged records?
- Things they did but never got caught for?

### Awards
- What have they been recognized for?
- Which recognition matters to them?
- Any they deserved but didn't get?

### Education
- Highest level completed?
- Favorite/least favorite subjects?
- Any significant teachers?
- Self-educated in any areas?

---

## SECTION F: FORMATIVE EXPERIENCES

### Infant experiences
- What do they know about their earliest years?
- Secure attachment or not?
- Any family stories about them as a baby?

### Young child experiences
- Key memories from ages 3-10?
- Moments of joy? Trauma?
- What did they want to be?

### Who was involved
- Parents: present, absent, loving, abusive, distant?
- Siblings: position in family, relationships?
- Extended family: who mattered?
- Non-family: teachers, neighbors, friends who shaped them?

---

## SECTION G: SEXUALITY & ROMANCE

### Sexual history
- First experiences?
- Patterns in relationships?
- Any trauma?
- Current relationship with sexuality?

### Lovers
- Significant romantic partners?
- What did each teach them?
- Any they still think about?
- Current status?

### Thoughts toward marriage
- Do they want to marry? Have they?
- Views on the institution vs. the relationship?
- Cultural expectations they accept or reject?

### Thoughts toward romance
- **Hopeful:** Believes in love, seeks connection
- **Cynical:** Love is a myth, partnerships are practical
- **Avoidant:** Intimacy is threatening
- **Ambivalent:** Desires connection but fears it
- How have past experiences shaped this?

---

## SECTION H: ADMIRATION & ENMITY

### People they look up to
- Living people they admire?
- Historical figures?
- Fictional characters?
- What qualities do these people share?

### People they hate
- Who and WHY?
- Is the hatred justified?
- Do they act on it or suppress it?
- What would it take to forgive them?

---

## SECTION I: INNER LIFE

### Dreams for the future
- What are they working toward?
- Secret ambitions?
- Dreams they've given up on?
- What would success look like?

### Fears
- **Surface fears:** Spiders, heights, public speaking?
- **Deep fears:** Abandonment, failure, death, insignificance?
- What's the fear behind the fear?
- How do fears manifest in behavior?

---

## SECTION J: PERSONALITY

### Personality traits
- List 3-5 dominant traits
- How do they see themselves vs. how others see them?
- Which traits are they proud of? Ashamed of?

### Favourite sayings
- Catchphrases?
- Verbal tics?
- Phrases they've picked up from others?
- What do these reveal?

### Habits
- Morning routines?
- Nervous habits?
- Comfort behaviors?
- Things they do without thinking?

---

## SECTION K: RELATIONSHIPS

### Relationships (general behavior)
- How do they act with strangers?
- Acquaintances? Friends? Close friends?
- Trusting or suspicious?
- Generous or protective?

### Family relationships
- Parents: alive? Relationship quality?
- Siblings: dynamics?
- Extended family: closeness?
- Chosen family?

### Problem relationships
- Who do they have unresolved tension with?
- Why hasn't it been resolved?
- What would resolution look like?

---

## SECTION L: LIFESTYLE

### Employment/School
- What do they do?
- How do they feel about it?
- Dream job vs. current job?

### Economic status
- Upper/middle/working class?
- Stable or precarious?
- How does money affect their choices?

### Economic behaviour
- **Frugal:** Saves, budgets, anxious about money
- **Lavish:** Spends freely, generous, impulsive
- **Strategic:** Invests, plans, money as tool
- What's their relationship with money?

### Pets
- Any animals?
- What does this reveal about capacity for nurturing?
- Past pets? Lost pets?

### Plants
- Do they keep plants?
- Can they keep them alive?
- What does this say about them?

---

## SECTION M: TEMPERAMENT ASSESSMENT

For each, consider: Is this always true? When does it change?

### Cheerful or Gloomy?
Default emotional weather

### Humorous or Violent?
Response to tension and threat

### Active?
Energy level, initiative-taking

### Sociable?
Drawn to people or solitude?

### Intellectual?
Engages with ideas? How?

### Virtuous?
Ethical tendencies, moral compass

### Expressive?
Shows emotions or hides them?

### Weakness
The crack in the armor. What could destroy them?

### Worries
What keeps them up at night?

### Unusual traits
What's weird about them? (This is often character gold)

### What is distinctive about them?
One sentence. The thing you'd remember.

---

## SECTION N: SKILLS & ABILITIES

### Fighting styles
- Trained or improvised?
- Philosophy of violence?
- Will they fight? When?

### Nimble?
- Graceful or clumsy?
- Quick reflexes?

### Sports/Dance
- Athletic abilities?
- Physical expression?

### Martial arts
- Formal training?
- Which style(s)?

### Guns
- Can they shoot?
- Own any?
- Feelings about weapons?

### Driving
- License? Skill level?
- Car person or not?

### Language study
- Native language(s)?
- Languages learned?
- Fluency levels?

### Other certifications
- Professional licenses?
- Unusual skills with credentials?

### Strengths
- What are they genuinely good at?
- What would others come to them for?

### Weaknesses
- Where do they struggle?
- What can't they do that frustrates them?

---

## SECTION O: HOBBIES, RECREATIONS, LIKES & DISLIKES

### Music
- Genres they love/hate?
- Specific artists?
- Do they play an instrument?
- Music at what times?

### Newspapers/News
- How do they consume information?
- Politically engaged?

### Books/Magazines
- Do they read? What?
- Favorite authors?
- Guilty pleasures?

### Movies
- Genres? Directors?
- Comfort films?
- Movie-watching habits?

### Creative pursuits
- Do they make anything?
- Art, music, writing, crafts?
- Skill level vs. enjoyment?

### Collections
- Do they collect anything?
- Why those things?
- Organized or chaotic?

### Favourite/Least favourite colour
- What colors do they gravitate toward?
- Colors they avoid?

### Perfume/Cologne
- Signature scent?
- Or scent-avoidant?

### Décor
- Minimalist? Cluttered? Themed?
- What does their space look like?

### Fashion
- How do they present themselves?
- Comfort vs. style?
- Distinctive pieces?

### Location
- Favorite places?
- Where do they feel at home?
- Dream destination?

### People
- Types they're drawn to?
- Types they avoid?

### Favourite stores/brands
- Brand loyalty?
- Shopping habits?

### Favourite things
- Objects they cherish?
- Why these things?

### Wears necklace/rings?
- Jewelry habits?
- Meaning of pieces?

---

## SECTION P: FOOD, CLOTHING, SHELTER

### Food likes
- Comfort foods?
- Cuisines?
- Specific dishes?

### Food dislikes
- Aversions?
- Allergies?
- Foods they'd never eat?

### Clothing preferences
- Style? Brands?
- Comfort vs. appearance?

### Shelter/Living situation
- House? Apartment? Other?
- Alone? Roommates? Family?
- Condition of their space?

### Taste
- Simple or sophisticated palate?
- Adventurous eater?

### Drugs
- Alcohol? Tobacco? Cannabis? Other?
- Relationship with substances?
- Conspicuous abstinence?

### Wine/Food pairings
- Gastronomic knowledge?
- Or completely uninterested?

---

## SECTION Q: HABITS & PASSIONS

### Habits
- Daily routines?
- Things they do automatically?
- Self-soothing behaviors?

### Favourite phrases
- Things they say often?
- Where did they pick these up?

### Meaningless things
- Trivial obsessions?
- Things they care about that "don't matter"?
- (OFTEN THE RICHEST CHARACTER DETAIL)

### Passions
- What lights them up?
- What would they talk about for hours?
- What do they lose time doing?

---

## SECTION R: THE UNCANNY

### Supernatural abilities
- Any powers?
- If this is a realistic story: any uncanny talents?
- If fantastic: what's their power and what does it cost?

### Attuned to spirits?
- Believe in ghosts?
- Spiritual sensitivity?
- Experiences with the numinous?

### Fortunes
- Lucky or unlucky?
- Sense of destiny?
- Relationship with fate?

### Accent/Dialect
- How do they sound?
- Regional markers?
- Code-switching?

### Sixth sense for certain things
- Intuitions that are uncannily accurate?
- What do they "just know"?
- How do they explain this?

---

## USING THESE PROMPTS

1. **Don't answer every sub-question** — Use them to spark ideas
2. **Follow interesting threads** — If one prompt opens something rich, go deep
3. **Notice contradictions** — They make characters interesting
4. **Trust "I don't know"** — Not every character needs every detail
5. **Return later** — You can always add more

---

*—The Araki Principle*
