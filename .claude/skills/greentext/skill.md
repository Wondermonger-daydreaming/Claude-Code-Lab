## The Format

```
>be me
>situation
>events
>punchline or revelation or twist
>mfw (my face when) or tfw (that feel when)
```

The greentext is:
- Compressed narrative
- Second-person imperative ("be me")
- Temporal sequence (arrow of events)
- Affective punchline (the feel)
- Anonymous (the speaker is "anon")
